Our History

TechCon started in 2005 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a premier conference, attracting industry leaders, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from around the world.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and explore the future of technology. We aim to bridge the gap between ideas and implementation to drive progress in the tech industry.

Notable Past Speakers

Here are some of the influential figures who have graced the TechCon stage:

  • Ada Lovelace: Known as the first computer programmer, Ada shared her insights on the future of AI at TechCon 2018.
  • Alan Turing: The father of modern computing discussed the importance of cryptography at TechCon 2017.
  • Grace Hopper: A pioneer in computer programming, Grace inspired audiences with her vision of computing's future at TechCon 2016.
